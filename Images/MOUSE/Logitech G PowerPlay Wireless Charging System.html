    A REVOLUTION
    IN GAMING

    With POWERPLAY and LIGHTSPEED technology integrated, Logitech G has solved the barriers to high performance wireless gaming. Input lag, wireless drops and dead batteries are now a thing of the past. Welcome to the future of wireless PC gaming, achieved through advanced science and unrivaled engineering.

    CHARGES WHILE AT REST AND AT PLAY

    POWERPLAY creates an energy field above its surface that allows charging even while a mouse is in motion and delivers power without interfering with the precise measurement and data transmission of a high-performance gaming mouse.

     

    BUILT-IN LIGHTSPEED WIRELESS

    The POWERPLAY charging current and integrated LIGHTSPEED wireless receiver are both powered with one cable. This puts the LIGHTSPEED connection right next to your mouse for the fastest and most reliable connection and eliminates the need for an additional USB port for the wireless receiver.

    TRANSFERABLE POWERCORE MODULE

    Mice are charged via the POWERCORE module. To use a different mouse, move the module to another compatible product.

     

    CLOTH AND HARD GAMING MOUSE PAD INCLUDED

    Tune your performance to your mouse pad of choice. We include both our cloth and hard surfaces so you can customize and swap interchangeably. POWERPLAY will suit your style right out of the box.

     

    MEET THE POWERPLAY GAMING MICE

    POWERPLAY is compatible with many Logitech G mice, including G502 X PLUS, PRO X SUPERLIGHT 2, G502 LIGHTSPEED and G703. Mouse not included with purchase of POWERPLAY.

    GAME-DRIVEN RGB

    The G logo lights up in brilliant LIGHTSYNC RGB color that responds to in-game action in many titles. Use Logitech G HUB and set your lighting color and animations with up to 16.8 million colors. You can even synchronize lighting effects with Logitech G keyboards, speakers, and headsets for a unified look and feel.
